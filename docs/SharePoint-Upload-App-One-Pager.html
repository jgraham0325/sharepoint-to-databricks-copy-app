SharePoint Upload App

Problem:

  • Manual effort needed to get data from SharePoint to perform an audit
  • Data ends up on users’ laptops — security risk
  • Overly complex data movement is inefficient and prone to failures

Solution:

  • Simplify the process: transfer directly from SharePoint to a Databricks Volume
  • Provide a UI so users can do this easily
Before
SharePoint→ Download to laptop→ Angular app→ Public ADLS→ ADF→ Private ADLS→ Ext. volume→ Transform & analytics
After — all in existing app
SharePoint→ Browse & copy→ Volume→ Transform & visualise
